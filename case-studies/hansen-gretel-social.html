HANSEN & GRETEL — RETAIL, SOCIAL + CONTENT PRODUCTION

Retail · Social Media Coordinator · Content Production · 2023–Present · Rosebery, Sydney
Focus: in-store styling, social strategy, content scheduling, and Ownley Online BTS support.

Overview

Long-term role across retail, social media, and content production for an Australian luxury fashion label. Started in retail (2023), expanded to Social Media Coordinator (2024), and continue producing content and supporting Ownley Online shoots.

Retail (2023–Present)

  • In-store sales, styling, and customer experience.
  • Product knowledge and visual merchandising support.
  • Building client relationships and brand awareness.

Social Media Coordinator (2024)

  • Developed and executed social media strategies aligned with brand tone.
  • Content scheduling, caption writing, and campaign execution.
  • Analysed engagement metrics and provided insights to inform improvements.

Ownley Online BTS (2024–Present)

  • Drawing on low fi production and content rather than high budget shoots: agile, on the day capture and styling that feeds into social and Ownley Online storytelling.
  • Styling assistance for product and campaign shoots.
  • Behind-the-scenes content capture for social storytelling.
  • On-set coordination and asset organisation.

Skills shown

  • Brand tone awareness and visual storytelling instincts.
  • Reliable operations (planning, scheduling, delivering on time).
  • Comfort with metrics and iteration.
  • Versatility across retail, digital, and production environments.
