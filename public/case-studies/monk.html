MONK

Brand Identity / Packaging Concept

MONK is a concept wellness brand I designed that reimagines how people experience sweetness. The brand is built around the idea that natural sweeteners should feel like a premium lifestyle product, rather than a diet substitute or artificial alternative. Avoiding clinical or overly functional wellness language, MONK aligns its identity more closely with modern skincare and lifestyle brands, positioning monk fruit as a clean, desirable everyday ritual.

I have developed a logo and some identity elements to envision the product concept. Everything from color, text, aesthetic, brand, etc, has been speculatively conceptualised. I do not claim ownership of the photography used.
